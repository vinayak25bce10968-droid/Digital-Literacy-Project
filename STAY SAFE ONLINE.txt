STAY SAFE ONLINE

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
2. Enable 2-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
3. Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp
4. Always verify websites before entering personal details (check HTTPS 🔒)
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts
6. Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security risks


FINANCIAL/UPI SAFETY

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone (even bank officials)
2. Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering PIN
3. Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve random payment requests
4. Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending money


REPORTING CHANNELS

1. Immediately report on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
2. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
3. Inform your bank quickly to block further transactions
